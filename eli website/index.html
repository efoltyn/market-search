Free market data for AI agents.

Ask a question. Your AI pulls the data, runs the analysis, gives you an answer. Timeseries, snapshots, options, prediction markets, macro, SEC filings. Works with Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, or eli tui with OpenRouter.

Why Eli

01
Raw Data First.

Timeseries, not summaries. If Yahoo says $72.44, the model works from that number. No hallucinated prices.

02
Compute Locally.

LLMs can't do math. Eli fetches with Rust, writes Python to calculate correlations and returns, executes it locally.

03
Options as Signal.

IV at 147% tells you more than price. Put/call ratios, skew, max pain—positioning data that news doesn't capture.

04
Prediction Markets.

Kalshi odds are forward-looking. "56% Japan recession" beats yesterday's headlines. Read the future, not the past.
